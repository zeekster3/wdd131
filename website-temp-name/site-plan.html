Game Gallery Site Plan

Isaac Tenney

WDD 131

Overview

Purpose

To showcase and preserve unique programming projects and game-related creations from my childhood, as a portfolio piece and personal archive.

Audience

Friends and family who want to see my creative work, colleagues who are curious about my past programming projects, and potential employers who want to see my initiative and creativity beyond formal education.

Dynamic elements

I will use JavaScript to create a gallery carousel where users can cycle through screenshots of my programming feats. There will also be interactive buttons to reveal additional project details dynamically.
